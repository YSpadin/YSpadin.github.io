My story

Born and raised in Switzerland, I started my education at a young age as a childcare provider. After a few setbacks in life, I then made the decision to start a new chapter. Due to my great interest in technical things and the passion for video games since my childhood, I decided to go on the way of a Jedi "ehmm" I mean a game developer.

Since 2020 I study game programming BsC at the SAE in Zurich.
